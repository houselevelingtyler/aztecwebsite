STEP 1

Creating your Wallet

If you already have a wallet you can skip this step. To create your crypto wallet you can click the "Create Wallet" tab down below or you can watch the "Explainer Video" tab to watch a video on how to create your crypto wallet. You need this to buy RGV coin.

STEP 2

Buying Ethereum (ETH)

In order to purchase our coin you need to first follow a small process. Click the video to learn how to buy Ethereum or buy using the button next to it.

STEP 3

Convert to Matic (POLYGON)

Once you have ETH, you will then need to bridge it to Matic (POLYGON). This is because our tokens are on the Polygon network.

STEP 4

Swap Matic (Polygon) to RGV

Now that you have converted your Ethereum (ETH) to Matic (POLYGON), you will lastly need to swap the Matic (POLYGON) for Aztec Crypto Currency RGV. Click the "explainer video" button to learn how or click the other button to swap now. Once swapped you will now own Aztec Crypto Currency RGVtokens!

STEP 5

START NOW!

Since you have now learned the process of buying RGV coin, press the Start button in the bottom left of your screen to begin your journey in buying RGV crypto currency!
